ECD Project HMA — Mix Details

Mix Design Method: Marshall Mix Design Method, MS-2 Asphalt Mix Design Methods, Asphalt Institute, USA.

Asphalt Surface Course (SC)

Class and Grade of HMA: Grading D5 (12.5 mm nom), ASTM D 3515.
Ref. Standards: AASHTO T 245, USDOT FHWA FP-14, MPI(Guyana).
Percentage Asphalt by mass of total mix, Pb:  4.9%
Design Air Voids, Pa: 4.0%
Design Voids in Mineral Aggregate, VMA: 15.0%
Design Voids Filled with Asphalt, VFA: 73.6%
Design Max. Specific Gravity of the mix, Gmm: 2.744
Design Bulk Specific Gravity of the mix, Gmb, : 2.634
Moisture Susceptibility (Tensile Strength Ratio): 92.5%.

Asphalt Binder Course (BC)

Class and Grade of HMA: Grading D4 (19.0 mm nom), ASTM D 3515.
Ref. Standards: AASHTO T 245, USDOT FHWA FP-14, MPI(Guyana).
Percentage Asphalt by mass of total mix, Pb:  4.6%
Design Air Voids, Pa: 4.0%
Design Voids in Mineral Aggregate, VMA: 12.9%
Design Voids Filled with Asphalt, VFA: 73.9%
Design Max. Specific Gravity of the mix, Gmm: 2.763
Design Bulk Specific Gravity of the mix, Gmb, : 2.670
Moisture Susceptibility (Tensile Strength Ratio): 92.5%.
